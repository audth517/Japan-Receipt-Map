Japan Receipt Map

From 230201 to 250512

This project visualizes every receipt I collected across Japan, mapping spending patterns, movement, and categories across regions.

Each square represents a single purchase. The size of the square reflects the price, and the color indicates a category.

Receipts act as micro-time capsules—capturing not only what was bought, but when, where, and why. This map reconstructs my journey through the shapes, sizes, and distribution of spending.
